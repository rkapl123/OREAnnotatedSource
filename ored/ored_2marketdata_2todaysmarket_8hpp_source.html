65 //! Today's Market
66 /*!
67  Today's Market differes from MarketImpl in that it actually loads market data
68  and builds term structure objects.
69 
70  We label this object Today's Market in contrast to the Simulation Market which can
71  differ in composition and granularity. The Simulation Market is initialised using a Today's Market
72  object.
73 
74  Today's market's purpose is t0 pricing, the Simulation Market's purpose is
75  pricing under future scenarios.
76 
77  \ingroup marketdata
78  */
