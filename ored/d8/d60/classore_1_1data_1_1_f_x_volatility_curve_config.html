◆ Dimension

enum Dimension
strong

supported volatility structure types

For ATM we will only load ATM quotes, for Smile we load ATM, RR, BF or Deltas SmileInterpolation - currently suports which of the 2 Vanna Volga approximations, as per Castagna& Mercurio(2006), to use. The second approximation is more accurate but can ask for the square root of a negative number under unusual circumstances.
