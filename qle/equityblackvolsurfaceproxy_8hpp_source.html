35 //! Wrapper class for a BlackVolTermStructure that allows us to proxy one equity vol surface off another.
36 /*! This class implements BlackVolatilityTermStructure and takes a surface (well, any BlackVolTermStructure) as an
37  input. It also takes Handles to two EquityIndices (index and proxyIndex), where index is the 'EquityIndex' of the
38  underlying for the surface being constructed and proxyIndex is the 'EquityIndex' for the surface being proxied off.
39 
40  The vol returned from the new surface is proxied from the base, adjusting by the forward prices to match ATM:
41 
42  \f{eqnarray}{
43  \sigma_2(K,T) = \sigma_1(\frac{K}{F_2}*F_1,T)
44  \f}
45 
46  Where \n\n
47  \f$ \sigma_1 = \text{Volatility of underlying being proxied against}\f$ \n
48  \f$ \sigma_2 = \text{Volatility of underlying being proxied}\f$ \n
49  \f$ F_1 = \text{Forward at time T of the underlying being proxied against}\f$ \n
50  \f$ F_2 = \text{Forward at time T of the underlying being proxied}\f$ \n
51  \f$ T = \text{Time}\f$
52  \n
53 
54  Note: This surface only proxies equity volatilities, this is because we are forced to look up the equity fixings
55  using time instead of date and use the forecastFixing method in an EquityIndex. A more general class could be
56  developed if need, using Index instead of EquityIndex, if the time lookup could be overcome.
57 
58  */
