Detailed Description

DK CPI index.

Both CPI and HICP are defined by ISDA for Danish inflation https://www.isda.org/a/EoMDE/2008-inflation-defs.pdf and FpML supports both http://www.fpml.org/spec/coding-scheme/fpml-schemes.html#s5.105 However looking at thing on the internet, e.g. https://e-markets.nordea.com/research/attachment/15539 https://e-markets.nordea.com/api/research/attachment/70696 It appears that DK CPI is the most commonly used
