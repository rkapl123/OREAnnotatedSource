22 /*! Cash flow dependent on the average of commodity spot prices or futures settlement prices over a period.
23 
24  The cash flow takes a start date and an end date. The set of valid pricing dates is determined from and including
25  the start date to but excluding the end date. The cash flow amount is then the arithmetic average of the commodity
26  spot prices or next commodity future settlement prices on each valid pricing date times the quantity. The next
27  commodity future is determined relative to each pricing date so the settlement prices for multiple commodity
28  contracts may be involved in the averaging.
29 */
