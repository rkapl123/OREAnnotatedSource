32 //! Pseudo Fixings Manager
33 /*!
34  A Pseudo Fixing is a future historical fixing. When pricing on T0 but asof T and we require a fixing on t with
35  T0 < t < T then the QuantLib pricing engines will look to the IndexManager for a fixing at t.
36 
37  When moving between dates and simulation paths then the Fixings can change and should be populated in a path
38  consistant manager
39 
40  The FixingManager controls this updating and reset of the QuantLib::IndexManager for the required set of fixings
41 
42  When stepping between simulation dated t_(n-1) and t_(n) and update a fixing t with t_(n-1) < t < t(n) than the fixing
43  from t(n) will be backfilled. There is currently no interpolation of fixings.
44 
45  \ingroup simulation
46  */
47 class FixingManager {
